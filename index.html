Welcome message

We are pleased to welcome visitors to St Benedicts Primary School. This guest network is available to support meetings, events and professional visits while helping maintain a safe and secure digital environment for pupils, staff and guests.

Safeguarding statement

Safeguarding remains a priority at all times. Internet access on this network is filtered and monitored to help protect children, staff and visitors. Any attempt to access harmful, unlawful or inappropriate material, or to interfere with school systems, may result in immediate removal of access and further action where necessary.

Visitor acceptable use summary

This service is provided for reasonable internet access only. Users must not attempt to bypass filtering, scan or access internal systems, share offensive content, engage in unlawful activity, or use the connection in any way that could compromise the school’s safeguarding, security or reputation.
